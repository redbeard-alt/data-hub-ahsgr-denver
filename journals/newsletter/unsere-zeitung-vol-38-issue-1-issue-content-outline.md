## President's Message

Note: Draft, about 180 words. Expand to 600 to 800 words before final.

Our chapter has been through a lot in recent years—illness, COVID, rising costs, and the loss of familiar meeting places—but one thing has not changed: the desire to keep our heritage alive. As Shirley said on a recent phone call, "It doesn't matter if we've got 20 members or 200 members. We just need people that are enthusiastic and want to keep their heritage going." That spirit is what this Maifest issue and our Spring Maifest Potluck are all about.

In this newsletter you'll find an article on Maifest written by chapter member Bryan Hein, who has been working with Shirley and others to help the Denver Metro Chapter begin gathering again in ways that are affordable, accessible, and realistic for our members today. I hope you'll read his piece, think about what this chapter has meant in your life, and consider joining us—either in person at the potluck or by sharing a story or photo for future issues.
